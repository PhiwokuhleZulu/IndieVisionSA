Our success stories

Yasmin Cheng

With the support of an IndieVision SA grant, Yasmin Cheng brought her unique vision to life, highlighting South African talent both in front of and behind the camera. Yasmin is now in discussions with Gambit Films for her next project, continuing her mission to elevate South Africa's film industry globally.

Sipho Dlamini

Sipho Dlamini's abstract thriller Zoo captivated audiences, and with IndieVision SA's support, the film earned a Grammy nomination. Sipho's innovative storytelling and bold vision have solidified his place as one of South Africa's most promising filmmakers.

Kagiso le Roux

Kagiso Le Roux's sci-fi drama Out of Africa imagines an alternate South Africa without oppression. Premiering at the AUS & NZ South African Film Festival, it is receiving glowing reviews and poised for major success.
