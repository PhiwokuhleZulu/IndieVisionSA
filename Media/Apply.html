Apply to IndieVision SA

How to Apply

Joining IndieVision SA is your first step towards bringing your cinematic vision to life. Follow these steps to apply:

  1. Review the eligibility criteria below
  2. Prepare your project proposal and supporting documents
  3. Fill out our online application form
  4. Submit your application and wait for our response

Eligibility Criteria

  • Must be a South African citizen or permanent resident
  • Age 18-35 at the time of application
  • Project must be set and filmed within South Africa
  • Applicants must not have previously directed or produced a feature-length film

What We're Looking For

IndieVision SA aims to support projects that:

  • Showcase unique South African stories and perspectives
  • Demonstrate innovation in storytelling or filmmaking techniques
  • Have the potential for social impact or cultural significance
  • Show feasibility within the proposed budget and timeline

Application Process

Our application process consists of the following stages:

  1. Initial Submission: Online application and project proposal
  2. Review: Evaluation by our panel of industry experts
  3. Interview: Shortlisted candidates will be invited for an interview
  4. Final Selection: Successful applicants will be notified
