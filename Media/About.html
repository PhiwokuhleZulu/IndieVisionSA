Indie Vision South Africa



The leading fund empowering the youth of the film industry in South Africa

IndieVision SA is more than just a funding organization; we are a movement dedicated to reshaping the South African film industry. By investing in the next generation of filmmakers, writers, and actors, we aim to tell the untold stories of our nation.


Our Mission

Our mission is to discover and nurture South Africa's hidden talents. We offer financial support, mentorship, and industry connections to ensure that our participants can bring their visions to life. By ensuring that all funded projects are set and filmed within South Africa, we create opportunities that directly benefit our local film industry and contribute to its growth.

What We Offer

IndieVision offers a range of support mechanisms, including:

  1. Funding Programs: We provide grants for project development, production assistance, and post-production support.
  2. Mentorship: Connect with industry veterans who offer guidance, feedback, and insights into navigating the film industry.
  3. Networking opportunities: Through partnerships with film festivals and production companies, we help you build connections that can advance your career.

Elegibility Requirments

To applly for funding, applicants must meet the following criteria:

  • Must be a South African citizen
  • Projects must be set and filmed within South Africa.
  • Applicants must submit a detailed project proposal, including a script, budget, and timeline.
  • Priority is given to projects that showcase South African stories and culture, with a focus on originality and social impact.

How it Works

Submit your project proposal through our online portal. Aplications are reviewed by a panel of industry expers who assess the projects' creativity, feasability, and potential impact. If selected, you'l receive financial support tailored to your project's needs, along with access to mentorship and industry connections. We collaborate with top production companies and festivals to ensure your project gets the exposure it deserves. Once your project is completed, we assist with distibution strategies, ensuring your work reaches a wide audience both locally and internationally.

Funding Sources

IndieVision SA is funded through a combination of public and private donations, corporate sponsorships, and partnerships with film organizaitons. Our supporters share our vision of fostering South African talent and are committed to helping us achieve our goals.


Our Collaborators

We work closely with industry leaders to provide our participants with the best opportunities. Our collaborators include:

  • Gambit films: A renowned production company specializing in films, television, and branded content.
  • Burnt Onion Productions: A leading television production company in gauteng.
  • The Film Factory South Africa: A prominent player in South African film production.
  • South African Film Festival: An international festival dedicated to showcasing South Arican cinema and supporting our alumni.
